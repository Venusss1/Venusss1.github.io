This research project is conducted at **Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen)**, supervised by **Prof. Yangyang Xu**.

### Motivation

Existing video generation models often produce outputs that violate physical laws and common-sense realism. This project addresses this by injecting domain knowledge retrieved from **real video datasets** into the generation process via a novel **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** framework.

### Approach

- Retrieve physically plausible video clips relevant to the target generation prompt
- Extract and encode transferable knowledge (motion patterns, physical dynamics) from retrieved clips
- Inject retrieved knowledge into the video generation model to improve physical realism

### Research Directions

- Multimodal LLM for video understanding
- Controllable video generation
- AIGC (AI-Generated Content)
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
